2. Ensure you have internet connection
3. Try switching AI providers in settings

#### **Code Not Generating?**
1. Make sure your prompt is clear and specific
2. Check that the AI panel shows "Connected to [provider]"
3. Try a simpler prompt first

1. Switch to Groq for faster responses
2. Use shorter, more specific prompts
